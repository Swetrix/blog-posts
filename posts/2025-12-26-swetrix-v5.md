## üë• User Profiles

One of the biggest challenges in privacy-friendly analytics is understanding user journeys across multiple sessions without invasive tracking. Swetrix v5 solves this with our new **Profiles** feature.

You can now analyse long-term user behaviour and link distinct sessions to anonymous user profiles. This allows for much more accurate retention analysis and user journey mapping.

**How does it stay private?**
We built this with a privacy-first architecture. We **do not** use cookies, and we **do not** store raw IP addresses for profile tracking. Instead, we use a combination of non-identifiable signals (like user agent) and a salt to generate a completely irreversible identifier (a similar mechanism to how we track sessions, but with a different, longer living salt).

For those who need even more precision (and have user consent), we've added the ability to pass your own custom User IDs, giving you the most accurate analysis possible for logged-in users.

## üí∞ Revenue Analytics: Connect Traffic to Value

Traffic is great, but revenue is what sustains a business. Swetrix v5 introduces comprehensive **Revenue Analytics**.

You can now connect your **Stripe** or **Paddle** (other payment providers are coming soon) accounts directly to Swetrix. Our SDK allows you to attribute payments to specific sessions and users.

- **Visualise your growth:** See revenue and refund analytics in clear, easy-to-understand charts.
- **Deep attribution:** Understand exactly _which_ sessions lead to purchases. See where your paying users came from and what actions they took on your website before converting.

Revenue analytics integrates with our other features like Profiles or Sessions, allowing for even more insights.

## üõ°Ô∏è Reborn CAPTCHA

Something we built years ago was CAPTCHA. It was quite a simple solution - your users needed to solve a basic puzzle to prove they are human - but it was poorly executed.

Puzzles, even more complex ones, can now be easily solved by AI, and they're very annoying to legitimate users, and an even worse nightmare for accessibility.

So we're rolling out a new CAPTCHA solution that is much more user-friendly and privacy-friendly, and does not require your users to solve a puzzle.

Instead, it's based on a proof-of-work algorithm that requires your users computers to do some math to prove they are legitimate users.

Although it's still not 100% perfect in protecting your website from bots, it's a huge improvement over the old CAPTCHA. It's already included in all of our paid plans and is billed as 1 captcha completion = 1 event.
