## The Elephant in the Room: Privacy & GDPR

Let's not mince words: [Google Analytics is illegal in several European countries](https://swetrix.com/blog/google-analytics/is-google-analytics-illegal-in-the-eu) (like Austria, France, and Italy) because it transfers personal user data to the US, violating GDPR.

Google Analytics relies heavily on cookies and cross-site tracking to build profiles on your users for their ad network. By using it, you are actively participating in the surveillance capitalism economy.

Swetrix is **privacy-by-design**. We don't use cookies. We don't sell data. We anonymize IPs. You don't need those annoying "Accept Cookies" banners just for analytics when you use Swetrix.

## Complexity vs. Simplicity

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is notoriously difficult to use. It's an enterprise-grade tool designed for data scientists, not website owners. finding basic information like "how many visitors did I have yesterday?" requires clicking through multiple nested menus. Many businesses have to hire dedicated specialists just to interpret their GA reports.

Swetrix is built for **humans**. Our dashboard is clean, intuitive, and gives you the most important metrics at a glance. You can set it up in minutes and understand your traffic instantly, without needing a certification course.

## Data Accuracy: The Sampling Problem

When your traffic grows, Google Analytics starts **sampling your data**. This means they stop counting every visitor and instead take a small sample size and "guess" the rest. This leads to inaccurate reports where you might be making decisions based on rough estimates rather than reality.

Swetrix **never samples data**. Whether you have 100 visitors or 10 million, we count every single request. The numbers you see are 100% accurate, always.

## Performance & Error Tracking

Google Analytics is purely a marketing tool. It tells you _who_ came, but not _how_ they experienced your site.

Swetrix bridges the gap between marketing and engineering. We include:

- **Full traffic analytics:** Swetrix provides you with all the necessary traffic analytics, including page views, visitors, session duration, entry/exit pages, and more.
- **Performance Monitoring:** Track Core Web Vitals, page load times, and identify slow pages that are killing your conversion rates.
- **Error Tracking:** Automatically detect JavaScript errors that are breaking your site for users.
- **Alerts & Notifications:** Set up alerts for specific events, traffic spikes, or errors, delivered directly to your preferred channels.

Google Analytics has none of this. If your checkout button is broken, GA will just show you a drop in conversions. Swetrix will tell you _exactly which error_ caused it.
