Live visitors are visitors who are currently active on your website. Real-time analytics tools often show how many people are online right now, which pages they are viewing, where they came from, and sometimes their approximate location, browser, or device.

Live visitor data helps teams understand what is happening at this moment, not just what happened yesterday or last week.

## When live visitor data is useful

Live visitors are useful for:

- Product launches
- Newsletter sends
- Social media campaigns
- Paid ad launches
- Incident response
- Content going viral
- Checking whether tracking is installed
- Watching traffic during a conference or announcement

For example, after sending a newsletter, live visitor analytics can show whether people are arriving, which links they click, and whether traffic reaches the intended landing page.

## What live visitors can show

Depending on the analytics platform, live visitor reports may show:

- Current active visitors
- Current pages
- Referrers
- Traffic sources
- Countries
- Devices
- Browsers
- Events
- Goals

The most useful live visitor dashboards connect real-time traffic with meaningful actions. A traffic spike is interesting, but a spike in signups, errors, or checkout completions is more useful.

## How to interpret live traffic

Real-time analytics is best for monitoring and debugging, not long-term strategy. Live visitor counts can fluctuate quickly. Use them to spot immediate changes, then review historical reports to understand lasting impact.
